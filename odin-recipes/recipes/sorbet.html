Refreshing Lemon Sorbet Recipe

A light, tangy, and refreshing dessert perfect for hot summer days.

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Freeze Time: 4 hours

Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4-6 lemons)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare simple syrup: In a saucepan, combine water and sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring constantly until the sugar is fully dissolved. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  2. Mix ingredients: Once the syrup has cooled, add the freshly squeezed lemon juice, lemon zest, and a pinch of salt. Stir until well combined.
  3. Chill: Cover and refrigerate the mixture for at least 1 hour to ensure it's completely cold.
  4. Freeze: Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions until it reaches a smooth consistency. If you don’t have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a shallow dish and freeze, stirring every 30 minutes until smooth.
  5. Serve: Once fully frozen, scoop the sorbet into bowls and serve immediately or store in an airtight container for later.

Tips for the Perfect Sorbet

  • For extra smoothness, strain the lemon juice to remove pulp and seeds.
  • Want more tang? Add extra lemon zest to intensify the flavor.
  • Pair with fresh mint or berries for an elegant presentation.
